Before fill out the form for registering your car, here is the explaination of some fields.

Year of manufacture: This is the year in which your has been produced.

Car make/manufacturer: This is the company which has produced your car.

Car Model: This is the model/name of your car, but the system has grouped car names into different international groups of car names(models), any car can fit into these groups.

Car Category: This specifies the size of your car, and identifies it for people who can book it. For example, car for whole family is different from car for individual person

Car Trim: Car trim specifies ..... I will fill it out.

Chassis Number: This is your vehicle Identification Number(VIN) which differentiates your car model to another model, and it comes with your car.

Number of seats: This specifies the number of people that your car can carry.

Discount: This specifies whether you will give discount to clients who will be booking your car, and how much discount in percentage like 5%, this is optional you can leave it and add it later.

Car description: This includes the other notes or things you want to specify to your car which you didn't find in the form. Also you can add popular name of your car.
